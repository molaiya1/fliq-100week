Privacy, Data Protection & Student Safety

WealthWise Kids LLC is committed to the highest standard of privacy and data stewardship, particularly with respect to minors. This platform is designed with a zero-PII architecture: at no point does it collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). No student names, birth dates, ages, grade levels, gender identifiers, photographs, email addresses, device identifiers, IP addresses, geolocation data, or biometric markers are captured at any stage of the simulation experience.

All behavioral response data is recorded anonymously using a randomly generated session token that cannot be linked back to any individual student or household. Facilitators and educators receive only aggregated, class-level behavioral insights. No individual student's results, scores, or behavioral profile are ever surfaced, shared, sold, or disclosed to any third party under any circumstance. WealthWise Kids LLC does not engage in any form of student data monetization.

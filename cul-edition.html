The $100 Week™

How It Works

A 4-day financial behavior simulation. No right or wrong answers — just real choices, real pressure, and a score that reflects how you actually think about money.

💰
You start with $100
$100 for 4 days. Real situations, real pressure. Every dollar you spend or save is tracked against your profile.
📅
One scenario per day for 4 days
Each day brings a different financial situation — peer pressure, family obligations, opportunity costs, and first-paycheck decisions.
🧠
Your choices reveal your behavior
Every decision is measured across 4 behavioral traits: Impulse Control, Planning, Risk Awareness, and Resilience.
📊
You get a FLIQ Score™ at the end
Your score (0–8) and profile label show how you naturally respond to financial pressure — not what you know, but how you act.
🔒
100% private — no names, no accounts
No personal information is ever collected. Your data is anonymous by design and never sold or shared.

    Privacy, Data Protection & Student Safety
    WealthWise Kids LLC is committed to the highest standard of privacy and data stewardship, particularly with respect to minors. This platform is designed with a zero-PII architecture: at no point does it collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) about students. No student names, birth dates, ages, grade levels, gender identifiers, photographs, email addresses, device identifiers, IP addresses, geolocation data, or biometric markers are captured at any stage of the simulation experience. All behavioral response data is recorded anonymously using a randomly generated session token that cannot be linked back to any individual student or household.

Behavioral Trait Averages

The FLIQ Score™ framework measures four behavioral domains — each scored 0–2 based on how students responded to financial pressure in the simulation. These are not academic grades. They reflect decision-making tendencies.

💡 Reading these scores: 1.5–2.0 = Strength · 1.0–1.4 = Building · Below 1.0 = Focus Area. First-session scores in the Emerging range are normal — the value is in the pattern across all four traits.
